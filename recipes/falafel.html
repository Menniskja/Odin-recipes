Falafel

Description

Falafel is a food eaten all around the world. Originating in the Middle East, falafel is also prepared in parts of North Africa and has attained considerable popularity in the Western World, too.

There are many kinds of falafel. However, falafel is traditionally prepared from a mixture of either chickpeas or fava beans and various herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • mint
  • lemon zest
  • coriander
  • garlic
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • parsley
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut up herbs
  2. dice garlic
  3. Blitz herbs in food processor
  4. Add chickpeas and blend until you have attained a creamy consistency
  5. Heat neutral oil in frying pan until hot enough for frying (you can test this by placing the end of a wooden spoon in the oil - if bubbles form around the spoon, then the oil is hot enough
  6. Mould falafel mixture into balls w/ 1/4 cup measurement and fry evenly on both sides
  7. Remove from oil w/ slotted spoon. Place on paper towel to cool/drain of oil and sprinkle salt over
  8. Serve in pita bread w/ salad and sauce.
